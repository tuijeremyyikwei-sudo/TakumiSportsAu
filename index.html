About Takumi

Built by Players.
For Players.

Takumi Sports was born from a simple frustration: buying premium pickleball gear in Australia meant long waits, eye-watering import fees, and zero local support when things went wrong.

We partnered directly with Osaka World to bring you authentic, top-tier paddles shipped locally — with real warranty support from people who actually play the game.

Takumi (匠) is a Japanese word for artisan or master craftsperson. It's the standard we hold every product to, and the experience we aim to deliver to every customer.
